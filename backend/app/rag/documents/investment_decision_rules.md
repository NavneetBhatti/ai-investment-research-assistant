# Investment Decision Rules

Investment decisions are based on combining multiple signals:
- Valuation
- Momentum
- News sentiment
- Risk level

Buy:
- strong overall signals
- positive trend
- acceptable valuation
- manageable risk

Hold:
- mixed signals
- fair valuation
- uncertain direction

Avoid:
- weak signals
- negative sentiment
- high risk without strong upside

Insufficient Data:
- missing key information
- no reliable decision should be made

Important notes:
- Always consider the investor's risk level
- Avoid making decisions with incomplete data
