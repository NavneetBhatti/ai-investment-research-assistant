# Momentum Rules

Momentum measures how the stock price is moving.

Short-term price change is a simple momentum indicator.

General guidelines:
- Change > +2% → strong bullish momentum
- 0% to +2% → positive momentum
- -2% to 0% → weak or neutral momentum
- < -2% → bearish momentum

Important notes:
- Strong upward movement may indicate investor confidence
- Negative movement may indicate risk or uncertainty
- Momentum should not be used alone for decisions
