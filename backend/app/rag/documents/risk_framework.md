# Risk Framework

Risk measures how stable or uncertain a stock is.

Key indicators:
- Earnings Per Share (EPS)
- Market Capitalization

General rules:
- Positive EPS → company is profitable → lower risk
- Negative EPS → company is losing money → higher risk

Market Cap:
- Large cap (> $200B) → more stable
- Mid cap → moderate risk
- Small cap (< $2B) → higher risk

Important notes:
- Larger companies are generally safer
- Smaller companies may have higher growth but higher risk
