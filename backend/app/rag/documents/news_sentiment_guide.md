# News Sentiment Guide

News sentiment reflects how positive or negative recent news is.

Positive signals:
- growth, expansion, strong earnings, upgrade, record profits

Negative signals:
- loss, decline, downgrade, lawsuit, weak performance

General rules:
- Positive news → increases confidence
- Negative news → increases risk
- No news → neutral signal

Important notes:
- News sentiment should support or contradict other signals
- Strong negative news can override good fundamentals
